1 · Radar Ring

Concentric rings with tick marks; a radar sweep spins around the button while listening. Reads as "scanning/sensing."

2 · Liquid Glass Core

Frosted glass shell over a soft ember core, echoing the particle-burst canvas above it. Glow breathes when listening instead of a hard glossy sphere.

3 · Faceted HUD Bezel

Cut-corner octagon with detached corner brackets, matching the glitch-wordmark language. Brackets flicker on activation like a targeting reticle.

4 · Waveform Morph

Flat matte disc, icon dissolves into a live audio-bar equalizer the instant it's listening — the button itself becomes the waveform.
